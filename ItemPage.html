Wand of Snowballs

Wand, Very Rare

This wand has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast the Snilloc's snowball swarm (XGtE) spell (DC 15) from it. For 1 charge, you cast the 2nd-level version of the spell. You can increase the spell slot level by one for each additional charge you expend.

The wand regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand's last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand crumbles into a flurry of snowflakes and is destroyed.


Source: Homebrew.
